Well, we're certainly a bit late to announce this!

The Dark Forest and The Dark Cave are being officially ported to PC.

We're incredibly excited to announce that after nearly 3 years, the exciting world of The Dark Forest will finally make its way to PC with The Dark Forest Enhanced Edition.

The Dark Forest Enhanced Edition is the definitive version of the game, currently only on Windows.

This version of the game includes many new things, including:

  • Two new difficulties
  • Completely rebalanced gameplay
  • New Game+
  • Achievements

And more!

All of this is realized by a new in-house engine that the original games have been ported onto, which future Letopa games will utilize even further.
