The Dark Forest Enhanced Edition is finally here

May 29, 2025

Well, we're certainly a bit late to announce this!

The Dark Forest Enhanced Edition is out now, available on Windows, Linux, and MacOS.

Port Surprises!

Before launching the closed beta, our team put some work into making ports of Enhanced Edition for x86 Linux devices and M series Macs.

These two ports are 100% experimental, and we don't have the resources to support these ports. We've ensured that there are minimal bugs on both versions to make up for this.

Update Plans

Now that the game is out, we're planning to support the Windows versions of Enhanced Edition up until October.

Our team is quite small at the moment, and our current focus is fully behind the first release of LCORE (an open-source version of the engine powering this game). As such, we cannot promise a patch every month or new content, but we will resolve any critical bugs or other issues as soon as we learn of them.

What's Next?

Good question. As we said before, we're preparing LCORE for its launch in October, but there is something else we've been working on for quite a while now that we're very excited about.

Stay tuned, because you never know when we might make an announcement...
